<p><strong>The Digital Ultrasonic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Retro-Reflective Sensor</li><li>Through Beam Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retro-Reflective Sensor and Through Beam Sensor are two types of Digital Ultrasonic Sensors used in various industries. </p><p>Retro-Reflective Sensor functions by emitting sound waves that bounce off a reflector and return to the sensor to detect objects within a certain range. </p><p>On the other hand, Through Beam Sensor works by emitting sound waves from a transmitter to a receiver located opposite to each other. An object obstructing the path between the transmitter and receiver triggers the sensor to detect its presence.</p></p>
