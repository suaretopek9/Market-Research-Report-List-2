<p><p>Dock safety gates are essential in preventing accidents and ensuring employee safety in loading dock areas. There are three main types of dock safety gates: Fold safety gates, Lift safety gates, and Others. Fold safety gates are hinged gates that fold back when not in use, providing easy access for workers and equipment. Lift safety gates operate vertically, creating a barrier when raised and allowing access when lowered. Other types of safety gates include sliding gates, swing gates, and rotating gates, each offering unique features and benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
