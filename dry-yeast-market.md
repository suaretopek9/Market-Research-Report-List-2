<p><ul><li>Active Dry Yeast</li><li>Inactive Dry Yeast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry yeast market consists of two main types: active dry yeast and inactive dry yeast. Active dry yeast is a form of yeast that is alive and needs to be activated before use by being dissolved in warm water. It is commonly used in baking to help dough rise. Inactive dry yeast, on the other hand, is a form of yeast that has been deactivated and can be added directly to dry ingredients without needing to be activated. It is often used as a flavor enhancer in savory dishes. Both types are popular choices in the food industry for their convenience and versatility.</p></p>
