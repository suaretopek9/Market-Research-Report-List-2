<p><p>Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) equipment is a geophysical method that uses radar pulses to image the subsurface. It is commonly used in engineering, environmental, and archaeological applications to detect objects and structures buried beneath the ground. GPR equipment emits electromagnetic waves into the ground, which bounce back when they encounter changes in subsurface materials, providing a visual image of what lies beneath the surface.</p><p>The Ground Penetrating Radar Equipment Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period.
