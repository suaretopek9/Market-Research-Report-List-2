<p><ul><li>Incandescent</li><li>Fluorescent</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Under cabinet lighting is available in three main types: incandescent, fluorescent, and LED. Incandescent lighting is the traditional option, providing warm, soft light but with higher energy consumption and shorter lifespan. Fluorescent lighting is more energy-efficient and longer-lasting than incandescent, but can be too harsh for some users. LED lighting is the most advanced option, offering energy efficiency, long lifespan, and various color options. It is also cooler to the touch, making it a safer choice for under cabinet lighting.</p></p>
