<p><p>The Arc Flash Detection Market includes Fiber-Optic Sensors and Arc-Flash Relays. Fiber-optic sensors detect arc flash incidents by monitoring changes in light intensity caused by the arc flash. These sensors provide real-time detection and can quickly alert personnel to potential hazards. Arc-Flash Relays, on the other hand, are protective devices that detect arc flash incidents based on current and light intensity levels. They can quickly trip the circuit to mitigate the effects of the arc flash and protect equipment and personnel.</p></p>
