<p><p>Standard TNC, Reverse Polarity TNC (RPTNC), BNC, and Standard SMA are all different types of antenna coupler markets. Standard TNC connectors are commonly used in RF applications, while RPTNC connectors are designed to prevent mismating with standard TNC connectors. BNC connectors are popular in audio and video applications due to their quick connect/disconnect feature. Standard SMA connectors are designed for high-frequency applications and are commonly used in antennas and other RF devices. Each of these markets caters to specific needs and applications within the antenna coupler industry.</p></p>
