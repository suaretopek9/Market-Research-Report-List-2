<p><p>Children nightlights can be categorized into two main types: Bluetooth and non-Bluetooth. These nightlights often come with additional features such as timers, color changing options, and music playback. They are simpler in design and functionality, perfect for parents looking for a basic nightlight solution for their children.</p></p>
