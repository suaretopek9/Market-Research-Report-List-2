<p><p>The air chuck market can be categorized into two main types: single head and dual head. Single head air chucks consist of one outlet for connecting to an air hose, while dual head air chucks have two outlets for simultaneous inflation of two tires. Single head air chucks are commonly used for inflating individual tires, while dual head air chucks are preferred for vehicles with dual tires such as large trucks or trailers. Both types of air chucks are essential tools for quickly and efficiently inflating tires.</p></p>
