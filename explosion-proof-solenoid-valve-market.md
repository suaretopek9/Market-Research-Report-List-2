<p><strong>The Explosion Proof Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Three-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Four-Way Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Explosion proof solenoid valves are classified based on the number of ports they have. Two-way solenoid valves have one inlet and one outlet, allowing for simple on/off control of fluid flow. Three-way solenoid valves have one inlet and two outlets, enabling mixing or diverting of flow. Four-way solenoid valves have two inlets and two outlets, allowing for complex control of flow direction. Each type serves a specific purpose in applications where safety against explosions is crucial.</p></p>
