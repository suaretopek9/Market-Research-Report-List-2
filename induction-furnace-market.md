<p><p>Induction furnace market consists of two main types: Coreless Induction Furnace and Channel Induction Furnace. Coreless induction furnaces utilize an electromagnetic field to heat and melt metal without the need for a core. Channel induction furnaces, on the other hand, use a channel system to direct the flow of metal for melting. Both types of furnaces are widely used in the metalworking industry for their efficiency and precision in heating and melting metals.</p></p>
