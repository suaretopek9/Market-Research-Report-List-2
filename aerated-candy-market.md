<p><p>Aerated candy is classified into three main types based on the level of aeration: high aerated candy, medium aerated candy, and low aerated candy. High aerated candy has the highest air content, resulting in a light and fluffy texture. Medium aerated candy contains a moderate amount of air bubbles, providing a balance between texture and density. Low aerated candy has minimal air content, resulting in a denser and chewier texture. Each type offers a unique sensory experience for consumers looking for different levels of lightness and chewiness in their candy.</p></p>
