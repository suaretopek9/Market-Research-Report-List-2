<p><p>Portable Handheld VOC Monitors come in different types, such as PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor monitors. PID monitors use UV light to ionize gases, which can detect a wide range of VOCs. On the other hand, Metal-oxide Semiconductor monitors rely on a chemical reaction with the VOCs to produce a measurable signal. Both types of monitors are portable and handheld, making them convenient for on-the-go monitoring of volatile organic compounds in various environments.</p></p>
