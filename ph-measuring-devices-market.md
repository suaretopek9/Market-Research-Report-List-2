<p><p>PH measuring devices can be categorized into two main types: pointer and digital display. Pointer devices are traditional PH meters that use a needle or dial to indicate the PH level of a substance. They are often cost-effective and easy to use. On the other hand, digital display devices show PH levels numerically on a screen, providing more accurate readings and advanced features such as data logging and calibration options. Both types of devices cater to different user preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
