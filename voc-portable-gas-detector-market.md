<p><p>The VOC Portable Gas Detector market consists of various types of detectors, including the Photoionization Detector (PID) and Gas Sensitive Semiconductor detectors. </p><p>PID detectors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in order to detect their presence. On the other hand, Gas Sensitive Semiconductor detectors rely on semiconductor materials that change their electrical conductivity in the presence of VOCs.</p><p>Both types of detectors are commonly used in industrial settings to monitor air quality and detect potentially harmful VOCs.</p></p>
