<p><p>Tunable capacitors are used in various electronic devices to adjust capacitance values. Air gap tunable capacitors use air as the dielectric material between the plates. Vacuum tunable capacitors utilize a vacuum as the dielectric, providing high quality performance. Sf6 gas filled tunable capacitors use sulfur hexafluoride gas as the dielectric material, offering enhanced stability and reliability. Each type of tunable capacitor has its own unique advantages and applications, catering to different requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
