<p><strong>The VOC Detection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VOC detection equipment market includes various types such as PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors. PID instruments utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules and detect VOCs, providing real-time measurement with high sensitivity. Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors operate by detecting changes in electrical conductivity when exposed to VOCs, offering cost-effective and reliable detection solutions. Both types of equipment are widely used in industrial settings, environmental monitoring, and occupational safety to identify and measure volatile organic compounds in the air.</p></p>
