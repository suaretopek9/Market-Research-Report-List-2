<p><strong>The Automated Driving Control Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perception Control Unit</li><li>Decision Control Unit</li><li>Actuator Control Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Driving Control Unit market consists of three main types: Perception Control Unit, Decision Control Unit, and Actuator Control Unit. The Perception Control Unit is responsible for collecting data from various sensors to detect and interpret signals from the surrounding environment. The Decision Control Unit processes this information and makes decisions on how the vehicle should behave. The Actuator Control Unit then executes these decisions by controlling the vehicle's actuators such as brakes and steering. Each unit plays a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient automated driving.</p></p>
