<p><strong>The Embedded Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Programmable Logic Devices</li><li>Complex Programmable Logic Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Simple Programmable Logic Devices (SPLDs) are low-cost, entry-level embedded controllers that are easy to program and suitable for simpler applications. They are typically used in consumer electronics, toys, and home appliances. On the other hand, Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLDs) are more advanced and offer greater flexibility and functionality. They are used in more complex applications such as telecommunications equipment, industrial automation, and automotive systems. CPLDs are more expensive than SPLDs but provide higher performance and capabilities.</p></p>
