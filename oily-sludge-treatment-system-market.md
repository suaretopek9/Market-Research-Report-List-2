<p><p>Oily sludge treatment systems can be categorized into two main types: dehydration type and centrifugal type. Dehydration type systems use heat and pressure to separate water and oil from the sludge, while centrifugal type systems use centrifugal force to remove solids from the mixture. Both types are effective in treating oily sludge and are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, petrochemicals, and wastewater treatment. Each type has its own advantages and suitability for different applications depending on the specific requirements.</p></p>
