<p><p>Hospital bassinets market can be classified into two main types: Fixed Height Bassinets and Height-adjustable Bassinets. Fixed Height Bassinets are stationary beds that cannot be adjusted in terms of height. They are typically used for stable newborns who do not require frequent adjustments. On the other hand, Height-adjustable Bassinets can be adjusted to different heights, making it easier for medical staff to access and care for the newborn. This type is often preferred for premature babies or infants with medical conditions that require closer monitoring.</p></p>
