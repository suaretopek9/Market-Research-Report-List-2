<p><p>Corrugated sample cutters come in two main types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic cutters use air pressure to power the cutting mechanism, making them ideal for heavy-duty cutting jobs. On the other hand, electric cutters are powered by electricity and are known for their precision and ease of use. Both types cater to different market demands, with pneumatic cutters being popular in industries that require high cutting power, while electric cutters are preferred for their accuracy and efficiency in cutting corrugated samples.</p></p>
