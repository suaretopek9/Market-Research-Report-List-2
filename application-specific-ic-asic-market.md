<p><strong>The Application Specific IC (ASIC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Custom Design</li><li>Standard-Cell Based</li><li>Gate-Array Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application Specific IC (ASIC) market types refer to different design approaches for creating customized integrated circuits based on specific applications. Full-Custom Design involves designing every aspect of the circuit from scratch, offering maximum flexibility but requiring high cost and time. Standard-Cell Based design involves using pre-designed and characterized cells for common functions, offering a balance between flexibility and cost. Gate-Array Based design involves using pre-defined gates and metal layers for customizing the interconnect structure, offering faster time-to-market but less flexibility than full-custom design.</p></p>
