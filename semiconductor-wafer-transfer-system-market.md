<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Transfer System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Transfer</li><li>Vertical Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Semiconductor Wafer Transfer System is used to transfer wafers between different processing equipment in a semiconductor manufacturing facility. In Horizontal Transfer systems, wafers move in a horizontal plane between machines, while in Vertical Transfer systems, wafers move in a vertical direction. Horizontal Transfer systems are preferred for their compact footprint and high throughput capabilities, while Vertical Transfer systems are used when space is limited or when high precision alignment is required. Both types of systems play a critical role in ensuring efficient semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
