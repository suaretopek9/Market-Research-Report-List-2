<p><ul><li>Conventional Lifeboat</li><li>Freefall Lifeboat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-righting lifeboat market consists of two main types: conventional lifeboats and freefall lifeboats. Conventional lifeboats are designed to be launched from a ship's davits and have a stable design that allows them to self-right if overturned in rough seas. Freefall lifeboats, on the other hand, are specially designed to be released from a greater height, offering a quicker and more controlled descent into the water in emergency situations. Both types of lifeboats play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of crew and passengers during maritime emergencies.</p></p>
