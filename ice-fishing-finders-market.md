<p><p>Ice fishing finders can be categorized into two main types: wired and wireless. On the other hand, wireless ice fishing finders use Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit sonar data to a mobile device or handheld display unit, offering more flexibility and freedom of movement. Both types of finders are popular among ice fishing enthusiasts, with each offering unique advantages and features.</p></p>
