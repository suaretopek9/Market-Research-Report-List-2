<p><strong>The RO Water Dispenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU RO Water Dispenser</li><li>POE RO Water Dispenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RO water dispensers are classified into two main types: POU (Point of Use) and POE (Point of Entry). </p><p>POU RO water dispensers are installed directly at the point of use, such as a kitchen sink, providing purified water on demand. This type of dispenser is commonly used for residential applications.</p><p>On the other hand, POE RO water dispensers are connected to the main water supply line to treat water at the point of entry into the building. These dispensers are suitable for commercial or industrial settings where a larger volume of purified water is required.</p></p>
