<p><p>Semiconductor Packaging and Test Equipment market consists of various types of equipment used in the semiconductor manufacturing process. Wafer Probe Stations are used to test the electrical characteristics of semiconductor wafers. Die Bonders are used to attach semiconductor chips to a substrate. Dicing Machines are used to separate individual semiconductor chips from a wafer. Test Handlers are used to automate the testing process of semiconductor devices. Sorters are used to categorize and package tested semiconductor devices based on their specifications.</p></p>
