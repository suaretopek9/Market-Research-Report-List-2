<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BEOL</li><li>FEOL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer market can be divided into Back-End-Of-Line (BEOL) and Front-End-Of-Line (FEOL) markets. BEOL refers to the processing steps that come after the transistors have been formed on the wafer, such as interconnects and metal layers. FEOL, on the other hand, includes the processes involved in creating the transistors themselves, such as doping and etching. Both markets are essential in the semiconductor industry, with BEOL focusing on improving performance and connectivity, while FEOL concentrates on enhancing the functionality of the transistors.</p></p>
