<p><p>The Non-Woven Adhesive Tape market consists of three main types based on the adhesive used: Acrylic, Rubber, and Silicone. Acrylic adhesive tapes offer strong bonding properties and are suitable for various surfaces. Rubber adhesive tapes have good initial tack and flexibility, making them ideal for applications requiring conformability. Silicone adhesive tapes are known for their high temperature resistance and are often used in industries where extreme heat or cold is a factor. Each type of adhesive tape caters to specific needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
