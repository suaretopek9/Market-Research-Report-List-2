<p><p>VOC gas meters are used to detect and measure volatile organic compounds in the air. Two common types of VOC gas meters are PID (photoionization detector) and metal-oxide semiconductor sensors. PID meters use ultraviolet light to detect VOCs, while metal-oxide semiconductor sensors detect changes in electrical conductivity caused by VOCs. Both types of meters are commonly used in industrial settings, environmental monitoring, and indoor air quality testing to ensure safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
