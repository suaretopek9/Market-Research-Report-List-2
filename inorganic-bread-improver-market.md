<p><p>Inorganic bread improvers include various types such as emulsifiers, enzymes, oxidizing agents, reducing agents, and others. Emulsifiers help stabilize the dough, enzymes improve dough handling and fermentation, oxidizing agents strengthen the dough structure, reducing agents improve dough extensibility, while other agents such as preservatives or dough conditioners may also be used. Each type plays a specific role in improving the quality, texture, and shelf life of bread products, catering to different market demands and consumer preferences.</p></p>
