<p><p>Rotary lobe vacuum pumps are categorized into two main types: oil-less rotary vane and oil-lubricated rotary vane. Oil-less rotary vane pumps do not require oil for lubrication, making them suitable for applications where oil contamination is a concern. On the other hand, oil-lubricated rotary vane pumps use oil for lubrication, offering better sealing and higher vacuum levels. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries based on specific requirements for cleanliness, performance, and maintenance.</p></p>
