<p><p>Lead ion selective electrodes are devices used for the detection and measurement of lead ions in a solution. There are two main types of lead ion selective electrodes: Crystal membrane and PVC membrane. Crystal membrane electrodes utilize a crystal material that selectively binds to lead ions, while PVC membrane electrodes use a polyvinyl chloride membrane that selectively allows lead ions to pass through. Both types of electrodes offer accurate and precise measurement of lead ion concentrations in various applications.</p></p>
