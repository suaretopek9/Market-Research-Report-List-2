<p><strong>The Enclosed Gearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaft Mounted Reducers</li><li>In-Line/Concentric Reducers</li><li>Off-Set/Parallel Reducers</li><li>Right Angle Reducers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enclosed gearing market types include shaft-mounted reducers, in-line/concentric reducers, off-set/parallel reducers, and right angle reducers. Shaft-mounted reducers are mounted directly onto the input shaft of the driven equipment. In-line/concentric reducers have the input and output shafts aligned in a straight line. Off-set/parallel reducers have the input and output shafts not aligned. Right angle reducers have the input and output shafts positioned at a 90-degree angle. Other market types may include custom or specialty enclosed gearing solutions tailored to specific industry needs.</p></p>
