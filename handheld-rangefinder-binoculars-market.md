<p><strong>The Handheld Rangefinder Binoculars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roof Prism Rangefinder Binoculars</li><li>Porro Prism Rangefinder Binoculars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld rangefinder binoculars are available in two main types: Roof Prism and Porro Prism. Roof Prism rangefinder binoculars are more compact and lightweight, making them convenient for outdoor activities. They have a straight-barrel design, with their objective lenses and prisms aligned in a straight line. Porro Prism rangefinder binoculars, on the other hand, have a traditional offset-barrel design, providing better depth perception and a wider field of view. They are bulkier but offer superior image quality and are often preferred by hunters and birdwatchers.</p></p>
