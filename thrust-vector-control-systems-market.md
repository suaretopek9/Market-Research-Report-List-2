<p><strong>The Thrust Vector Control Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical</li><li>Electrohydraulic</li><li>Other Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thrust Vector Control Systems market can be categorized into three main types: Electromechanical, Electrohydraulic, and Other Systems. Electromechanical TVC systems use electric motors to drive the actuators which control the direction of thrust. Electrohydraulic TVC systems use hydraulic power to move the actuators. Other Systems include pneumatic and all-electric TVC systems which use alternative methods to manipulate thrust direction. Each type of TVC system has its own advantages and drawbacks, making them suitable for different applications in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
