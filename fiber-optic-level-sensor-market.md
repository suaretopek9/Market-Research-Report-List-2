<p><p>Fiber optic level sensors are devices used to measure the level of liquids or solids in various industrial applications. There are two main types of fiber optic level sensors: contact and contactless. Contact sensors come in direct contact with the substance being measured, while contactless sensors utilize light signals to measure the level without physically touching the substance. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the application, such as accuracy, reliability, and environmental factors.</p></p>
