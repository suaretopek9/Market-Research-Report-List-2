<p><p>Thyristor Surge Protection Devices (TSPD) come in two main types - Bidirectional TSPD and Unidirectional TSPD. Bidirectional TSPDs are capable of conducting surge currents in either direction, making them suitable for protecting equipment from both positive and negative voltage transients. On the other hand, Unidirectional TSPDs are designed to only conduct surge currents in one direction, typically used for protecting against either positive or negative voltage transients. Both types of TSPDs are essential in safeguarding electronic equipment from damaging voltage spikes.</p></p>
