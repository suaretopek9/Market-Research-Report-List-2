<p><ul><li>Pulse</li><li>Analog</li><li>Network</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Servo drive systems are commonly classified into three main types: pulse, analog, and network. Pulse servo drives use digital signals to control the motor position and speed, offering precise and accurate motion control. Analog servo drives utilize analog voltage signals for motor control, providing smooth and continuous motion. Network servo drives are connected to a network for communication and control, enabling advanced features such as remote monitoring and diagnostics. Each type of servo drive system caters to different industry requirements and applications.</p></p>
