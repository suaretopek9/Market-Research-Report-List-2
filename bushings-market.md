<p><p>Bushings are crucial components in power transformers, providing insulation and support for electrical conductors. There are three main types of bushings based on insulation material - porcelain, paper, and resin. Porcelain insulation bushings are known for their high mechanical strength and reliability. Paper insulation bushings are cost-effective and commonly used in distribution transformers. Resin insulation bushings offer excellent resistance to moisture and contaminants. Each type of bushing has its advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and application needs.</p></p>
