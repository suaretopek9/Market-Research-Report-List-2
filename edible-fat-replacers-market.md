<p><p>Edible Fat Replacers market can be categorized into three main types: Protein-based Fat Replacers, Carbohydrate-based Fat Replacers, and Fat-based Fat Replacers. Protein-based fat replacers are derived from plant or animal proteins and provide a creamy texture similar to fats. Carbohydrate-based fat replacers are typically made from starches or fibers and mimic the mouthfeel of fats. Fat-based fat replacers are often made from vegetable oils or fats and provide the same richness and mouthfeel as traditional fats, while reducing overall calorie content.</p></p>
