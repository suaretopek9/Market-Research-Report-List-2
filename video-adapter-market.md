<p><ul><li>Integrated Graphics</li><li>Discrete Graphics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video adapter market consists of two main types: integrated graphics and discrete graphics. Integrated graphics are built into the computer's motherboard, providing basic graphics capabilities suitable for everyday tasks. Discrete graphics, on the other hand, are standalone graphics cards that offer higher performance and are preferred by gamers and professionals for tasks like video editing and graphic design. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, with integrated graphics being more common in budget-friendly or basic computing devices, while discrete graphics cater to users seeking advanced graphics performance.</p></p>
