<p><ul><li>Digital Optical Liquid Level Switches</li><li>Analog Optical Liquid Level Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Liquid Level Switches come in two main types - Digital and Analog. </p><p>Digital Optical Liquid Level Switches use non-contact technology to detect liquid levels and provide a simple on/off signal. They are often used in applications where precise measurements are required. </p><p>Analog Optical Liquid Level Switches, on the other hand, provide continuous level monitoring by converting the sensor's optical signal into an analog output. They are suitable for applications where real-time monitoring and control of liquid levels are necessary.</p></p>
