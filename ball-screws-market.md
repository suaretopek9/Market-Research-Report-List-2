<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ballscrew</li><li>External Circulation Ballscrew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screws are precision mechanical devices used to convert rotational motion into linear motion. There are two main types of ball screws: Internal Circulation Ball screw, where the balls circulate within the nut and screw assembly, and External Circulation Ball screw, where the balls circulate in a separate tube around the screw. Internal circulation ball screws are more compact and provide higher precision, while external circulation ball screws are better suited for high load applications and longer travel distances. Both types offer high efficiency and accuracy in various industrial applications.</p></p>
