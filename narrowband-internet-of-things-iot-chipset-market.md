<p><p>Narrowband Internet-of-Things (IoT) Chipset market types are categorized as Stand-Alone, Guard Band, and In-Band market. Stand-Alone refers to the use of separate frequency bands distinct from existing cellular networks for IoT devices. Guard Band involves utilizing unused frequency bands to prevent interference with existing networks. In-Band market operates within the same frequency bands as traditional cellular networks, allowing for more efficient use of spectrum. Each type offers different advantages in terms of network connectivity, interference management, and spectrum utilization.</p></p>
