<p><p>The life-saving buoy market consists of various types of buoyant devices designed to save lives in emergency situations. Rescue cans are buoyant devices shaped like a large can, which can be used to assist swimmers in distress. Life floats are larger floating devices that can support multiple individuals in the water.
