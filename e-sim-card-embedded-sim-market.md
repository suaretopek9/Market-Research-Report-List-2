<p><p>The E-SIM Card, also known as the Embedded SIM, comes in two market types: SMD (Surface-Mounted Device) and SIP (System in Package). SMD E-SIM cards are surface-mounted directly onto the circuit board, enabling smaller and more compact designs. On the other hand, SIP E-SIM cards are packaged within a module that can be easily integrated into various devices. Both market types offer the advantages of remote SIM provisioning, streamlined manufacturing processes, and increased flexibility for device manufacturers.</p></p>
