<p><p>Commercial ventilation systems come in three main types: exhaust, supply, and balanced ventilation systems. Exhaust ventilation systems work by removing stale air from a building, while supply ventilation systems bring fresh air in. Balanced ventilation systems combine elements of both exhaust and supply systems to maintain a healthy indoor environment. Each type of system has its own advantages and is used in different settings depending on the specific ventilation needs of the building.</p></p>
