<p><strong>The Protein Production Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prokaryotic Expression Systems</li><li>Mammalian Cell Expression Systems</li><li>Yeast Cell Expression Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protein production services encompass a range of market types, including Prokaryotic Expression Systems, Mammalian Cell Expression Systems, Yeast Cell Expression Systems, and Other systems. Prokaryotic systems, such as E. coli, are often used for high-throughput protein production due to their cost-effectiveness. Mammalian cell systems, like CHO cells, are preferred for producing complex proteins that require proper folding and post-translational modifications. Yeast cell systems, such as Saccharomyces cerevisiae, are known for their scalability and ability to produce recombinant proteins efficiently. Other systems, including insect and plant cells, offer specialized solutions for specific protein production needs.</p></p>
