<p><p>The Diesel Injection System market consists of two main types: Direct Diesel Injection System and Indirect Diesel Injection System. Direct Diesel Injection System involves injecting fuel directly into the combustion chamber, resulting in better fuel efficiency and lower emissions. On the other hand, Indirect Diesel Injection System involves injecting fuel into a pre-combustion chamber, which then flows into the main combustion chamber. Each type has its own advantages and drawbacks, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
